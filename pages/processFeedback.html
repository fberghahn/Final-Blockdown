Contribute

As this project is published and the academic process concluded, we're committed to continuing the participatory process. We'll be sharing unimplemented feedback and outstanding requests from our test users. This is your chance to find out where and how you can join and contribute to the development of this project.

This step is designed to spark your interest, make it easier for you to get involved, and bring more participants into the participatory process. We're excited to see how you can help us grow and improve!

Here is a Guide to Contribution so you know the preferred process
